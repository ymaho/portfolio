Pretty Flappy Birdy

Developed a flappy bird game with dynamic leaderboard and scoring system as one of my team projects.

The game is simple: press the space button to help the bird to survive longer than other players to win. Crashing into objects or falling on the ground will be game over. Since the game is full-stuck developed, users are allowed to see othersâ€™ real-time score which makes them more competitive.

The aesthetic design is to increase their enjoyment as well as for marketing purpose. We believe the users are more likely to share the experience on social media platforms when it is "intagramable". When the users do so, the game would have free-advertisements o large range of user types, and potentially increase user amount.
